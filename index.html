Introduction

Hello, I'm Sean Daniel Helman or S. D. Helman, but you may call me Sean or Dan (I acknowledge both). I am also applying to the PhD program in Literature & Criticism at IUP. I currently reside at the bottom of Westmoreland County and particularly enjoy the rolling hills and forest patches of the area while being in the foothills.
